iCendant Chat is an "appless" empathetic AI chat that you treat just like any other contact in your address book. You can
reach out any time when your other friends and family are not available.

You do not need to download an app, sign up for an account beforehand, or provide any personal information.

iCendant Chat is free for up to 8 messages a day and 40 messages a month. After that, it is just $4.95 a month for
50 interactions a day and 250 per month.

Just start texting with (206) 898-9507. Message and data rates may apply. Unsolicited text messages are not
sent to your number.  iCendant Chat only responds to your text messages. Not intended for children under the age of 13.
Service only available in United States of America. iCendant Chat is not a replacement for emergency or mental health
services. If you are in crisis, please call 911 or your local emergency number.
