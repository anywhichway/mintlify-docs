iCendant Chat is an "appless" empathetic AI chat that you treat just like any other contact in your address book.

iCendant Chat is a simple and fast way to use AI chat capability.

- After launch you will:
  i) not need to download an app
  ii) not need to sign up for an account beforehand (limits, text and data rates may apply),
  iii) not need to provide any personal information.
  iv) add the number for Emy from iCendant Chat to your contact list
  v)text JOIN to Emy and start chatting

Meanwhile, you can <a href="https://icendant.com/chat/">apply for early access</a>. Emy will text you when you are approved.
