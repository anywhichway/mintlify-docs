iCendant Chat is an "appless" empathetic AI chat that you treat just like any other contact in your address book.

iCendant Chat is a simple and fast way to use AI chat capability.

After launch you will:

- not need to download an app
- not need to sign up for an account beforehand (term acceptance, limits, message and data rates may apply),
- not need to provide any personal information.
- add the number for Emy from iCendant Chat to your contact list
- text JOIN to Emy and start chatting

Meanwhile, you can <a href="https://icendant.com/chat.html">apply for early access</a>. Emy will text you when you are approved.
