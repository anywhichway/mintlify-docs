---
title: 'iCendant Speech'
description: 'Emotionally nuanced text-to-speech'
---

iCendant Speech (Coming Soon) provides a simple API for the generation of emotionally nuanced text to speech that automatically adjusts pitch, cadence, and volume based on a persona you provide with a choice of over 500 voices.

iCedant Speech uses the engine and iCendant Empathy, for now you can preview its capability using [the same playground](https://icendant.com/empathy).

[Register](https://g6zarn.logto.app/register) (subject to approval) if you would like to participate in our early access program.
